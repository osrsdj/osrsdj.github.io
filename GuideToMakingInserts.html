Guide to Making Inserts

Any foam can be used. Recommended is this Eva 50 x 50cm Black — one pack allows for 12 inserts to be made for the CTOMS Drug box.

You will need to cut out a bottom layer from the EVA foam. Some drugs will require the bottom layer to have a half cut so that they sit low enough.

Spray contact adhesive is recommended to glue the EVA together.

Cutting Settings

For cutting all the way through, use these settings:

Speed (mm/s)450
Power Max (%)90
Power Min (%)60
Pass count1

Half Cut Settings

For cutting half way through, used for drugs that are larger (e.g. TXA, Cefazolin), use these settings:

Speed (mm/s)150
Power Max (%)60
Power Min (%)40
Pass count1
Ensure it's set to fill instead of line.
